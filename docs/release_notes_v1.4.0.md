The mind-games update — **two new classes, and your abilities show up on everyone's screen again**.

## New class: Decoy

Press **J** for **Doppel**: a perfect copy of you — name tag, colors, even the IT arrow — takes off running the way you were going, while *you* fade out. On your own screen you're half-transparent so you can keep playing yourself; on everyone else's screen **you vanish completely** for 1.5 seconds. You're still taggable the whole time — the ability buys confusion, not safety. The clone turns at walls, and pops in a puff the moment an opponent touches it. (9s cooldown.)

## New class: Mason

Press **J** for **Ledge**: conjure a platform directly under your feet for 4 seconds. Catch yourself mid-fall, bridge the slush, turn any jump into high ground. It's a one-way platform like the map's transparent ones — jump up through it, drop through it — and **anyone can stand on it, the tagger included**. It blinks before it crumbles; don't be on it when it does. (8s cooldown.)

## Fixed: remote ability visuals were broken online

A practice-mode change had quietly disconnected the remote-VFX path in online matches — stun bursts, rewind telegraphs, the tag lunge streak, and tag hit-stop weren't showing on other players' screens. They're back. Reading the other player's ability is half the game; now you can again.
