Gameplay feel update — **a proper countdown start, and characters that lean into the hills**.

## Ready, set, GO

Rounds no longer wait for someone to land the first tag. Everyone loads in, gets a **5-second countdown** to spread out and find their footing, then the clock starts automatically with a big **GO!**. Tagging is off during the countdown, so nobody gets caught before the round really begins — it's a fair start every time.

## Characters bank on slopes

Stand on an angled platform and your character now **tilts to match the slope** instead of staying stiffly upright — a small thing that makes the ramps feel like real ground. It eases in smoothly, shows on every player's screen, and your name tag stays level so you're still easy to spot. (Decoys lean too, so they keep up the disguise.)
